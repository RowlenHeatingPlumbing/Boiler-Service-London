What's Included In A Boiler Service

We provide gas boiler services throughout London, ensuring the safety and well-being of your home and family. Getting regular maintenance guarantees your boiler’s reliability, preventing breakdowns and enhancing efficiency to minimise expenses while keeping your warranty valid. We also carry leading manufacturer boiler parts.

A routine boiler service with our Gas Safe engineers typically includes:

  • Identifying issues like leaks, failed or failing parts, corrosion, and other damages
  • Cleaning boiler components if necessary
  • Emptying the condensate trap
  • Examining the burner, heat exchanger, and spark electrode
  • Testing the gas pressure
  • Ensuring that there’s adequate ventilation
  • Checking the sealing of flue terminals and internal flue
  • Inspecting the boiler casing seal
  • Analysing burner combustion for efficiency
  • Providing a complete service report
  • Benchmarking records for future reference

Only by having your boiler serviced annually by one of our Gas Safe engineers can you be sure that you won’t suffer from dangerous emissions, expensive repairs and high energy bills.
